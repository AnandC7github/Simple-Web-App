The Ghost

  • "On dating apps, it's not just people you're swiping left on; it's the potential ghosts too. You develop a sixth sense for detecting spectral vibes in profile pictures. 'Oh, he's holding a disappearing act in his third photo. Swipe left!'"
  • "In the end, ghosting is like the sitcom of dating - you never know when it's going to end, and the laughs are usually at your own expense. But hey, we've all been haunted at some point. So, if you're ever feeling ghosted, just remember, it's not you, it's them. And who knows, maybe one day you'll find the ghost who's brave enough to stick around and share a hauntingly good love story."
